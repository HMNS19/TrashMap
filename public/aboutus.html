About Us

Key Features

Real-Time Geolocation

The system will utilize GPS to display the nearest available dustbins to users. This feature encourages users to dispose of waste properly by guiding them to designated disposal sites, reducing littering and promoting cleaner environments.

Black Site Reporting

Users can report "black sites" or locations where garbage accumulation is common, such as illegal dumping areas or overflowing bins. This alerts local authorities or waste management teams, enabling them to take swift action to clean these areas and prevent further waste build-up.

Garbage Pickup Scheduling

The platform will allow users to schedule garbage pickups from their homes or businesses, reducing the likelihood of missed collections. Additionally, the system will optimize waste collection routes based on user inputs, enhancing efficiency and lowering fuel consumption for garbage trucks.

Reward System

To incentivize responsible waste disposal, users will earn points each time they use the app's services, such as reporting black sites or properly disposing of waste. Once users accumulate 1,000 points, they can redeem them for rewards like AICTE points (for students and professionals) or other benefits. This gamification approach encourages continuous engagement and fosters a culture of sustainability.

Benefits of the System

Enhanced Community Cleanliness

With features like real-time geolocation and black site reporting, users will have an easier time disposing of waste properly, and authorities can respond quickly to garbage accumulation issues.

Promotes Responsible Behavior

The reward system not only encourages individuals to actively participate in proper waste disposal but also offers tangible incentives, motivating ongoing use of the system.

Environmental Impact

By integrating efficient waste collection methods and preventing litter through geolocation and reporting, the system contributes significantly to environmental sustainability efforts and supports cleaner, greener communities.
